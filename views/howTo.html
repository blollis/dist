How To

Lasic Lager

Steps

    1. Bring water to 180 degrees F.

    2. Steep malt in water for one hour.

    3. Bring solution to boil and boil for one hour.

    4. Add hops at timed intervals

    5. Cool and pleace in fermenter with yeast.

    6. Wait 3-4 weeks. Keg, carbinate, and drink.

Ingredients

    1. Malt

    2. Different Malt

    3. Yeast

    4. Hops
